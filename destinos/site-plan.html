Overview

Purpose

Destinos is a travel agency that sells only in person. The main objective of the website is to create an online presence that will allow the agency to reach out to different clients around the world. Destinos will be a reservation website for hotels, flights and cars.

Audience

Any adult capable of making online purchases will be able to use the Destinos website. The design of the website is expected to be eye-catching and the interface easy to use.

Hotels

Thanks to our extensive database, you will quickly find accommodations in any region of the world. The list at your disposal allows you to choose your accommodation based on price as well as available amenities. You can easily find a hotel or suite in a specific location. Search by city, country or proximity to a specific airport, select the date you are interested in and choose from the available options.

Flights

Indicate your destination and your airport of origin. DESTINOS will search through numerous airlines for the best cheap flight offers. After a few minutes the available flight offers will be displayed so that you can book and choose the one that suits you best.
